IRCC '26 Presentation

Prototyping & Flight Testing Biomimetic Wings

The Problem Aviation generates 1B tons of carbon annually. With demand projecting a 110% rise by 2050, innovative fuel efficiency solutions are critical to combat global warming.
The Hypothesis Implementing humpback whale flipper tubercles or slotted eagle primary feathers into aerofoil designs minimizes drag, thereby increasing lift-to-drag (L/D) ratios and fuel efficiency.
1

3D Modeling & CFD Analysis

Designed 10-inch half-wing models and conducted 204 CFD simulations across 15,000 trials (50-240 m/s). Built a custom wind tunnel with 280 PVC pipes for empirical laminar flow validation.

Peak Performance Data +47% L/D Ratio @ 50 m/s, 5° AoA
2

UAV Fabrication & Flight Test

Scaled to a 40.5-inch full wingspan and fabricated custom aircraft platforms. Programmed an onboard data logger to track telemetry during rigorous field tests at ~10 m/s.

Field Results (Distance/Volt) +12% Flight Range 1802 m/v vs 1596 m/v

Ion vs. Chemical Propulsion Study

Conducted a comparative study evaluating propulsion systems in modern spacecraft. Analyzed specific limitations, thrust-to-weight ratios, and long-term deep space benefits of each methodology.
